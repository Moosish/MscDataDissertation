Help & Documentation

Getting Started

How to create your first scouting view

To create your first scouting view, go to the Scouting page, select your filters such as statistics, age range, and league, and click "Create View". Your scouting view will be saved and appear in the list below.

How to set up your squad

On the Squad View page, you can manage your team's roster by adding or removing players. The players are categorized by their positions, and you can also update their details such as age, nationality, and injury status.

FAQs

What is the Live View feature?

The Live View feature allows you to monitor ongoing matches in real time, displaying key statistics and visualizing player positions on the field.

How do I enable dark mode?

To enable dark mode, click on the "Toggle Dark Mode" button in the sidebar. This will switch the theme of the entire application to a darker color scheme, which is easier on the eyes in low-light environments.

Troubleshooting

I can't see any live match data. What should I do?

If you're unable to see live match data, ensure that you are connected to the internet and that the match data provider is online. You may also need to refresh the page or try again later.

My scouting view isn't saving. What could be wrong?

If your scouting view isn't saving, check that you've filled out all the required fields in the Scouting form. If the problem persists, try clearing your browser's cache or contact support for further assistance.

Advanced Features

Customizing your notifications

You can customize your notifications by going to the Settings & Preferences page. Here, you can choose which types of notifications you'd like to receive, such as match alerts, scouting updates, and more.

How to integrate third-party data sources

To integrate third-party data sources, go to the Data Source Management section under Settings & Preferences. From here, you can connect to various external data providers and configure how the data is used within the platform.
